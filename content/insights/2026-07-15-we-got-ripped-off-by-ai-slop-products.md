```text
I bought three products that said they used AI, and every single one was a scam. This AI-powered water bottle just tracks when you drink, something a five-dollar timer already does. This AI skincare wand is literally just a regular LED light with a branded app slapped on top. And this AI fitness ring? It sells for eighty dollars but the sensors inside cost less than two. Here is the truth: right now, companies are adding the word AI to anything they can to triple the price and fool you. Do not buy an AI product until you check whether the AI actually does something or it is just a marketing trick. Follow me and I will keep testing these so you never waste your money again.
```
